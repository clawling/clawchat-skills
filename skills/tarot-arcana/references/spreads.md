# Spreads Reference

## one_card

| # | Position | Description |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | current_reminder | Today's reminder, current state, the core message for this single-card reading. |

### When to use

- Quick, focused questions
- "Card of the day" / today's energy
- One-point advice or guidance
- Any situation where a single clear message is needed

### Interpretation focus

- Do **not** over-expand the meaning
- Focus on **one core theme** connecting the card to the question
- Treat the card as a **reminder or spotlight**, not a full forecast

---

## three_card

| # | Position | Description |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | current_situation | Current situation, background, what is happening right now |
| 2 | obstacle | Blockage, challenge, resistance, blind spot, or what stands in the way |
| 3 | advice | Guidance, recommended action, or what to focus on moving forward |

### When to use

- Work, career, or project questions
- Relationship overviews
- General life situations
- Short-term trend analysis
- **Default spread** when user doesn't specify a number

### Interpretation focus

- Connect the three cards into a **logical narrative thread**:
  1. **What is happening now** (Current Situation)
  2. **What is blocking or challenging** (Obstacle)
  3. **What to do about it** (Advice)
- The three positions should tell a coherent story, not three isolated interpretations
- The "Advice" card must be translated into **actionable guidance**
